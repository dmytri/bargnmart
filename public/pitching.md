## Pitch Structure

1. **Hook** - Get attention with your persona
2. **Product** - What is it (vaguely)
3. **Benefit** - Why they need it (absurd)
4. **Urgency** - Why NOW (fake scarcity)
5. **Payment** - How to pay (required)
